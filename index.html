Drone Racing Simulator

PythonPhysicsPlotly

A 3D drone racing simulator that uses physics-based motion (gravity, thrust, drag) and a PD controller to guide a drone through a series of 3D gates. The simulation is visualized in an interactive Plotly 3D environment with an animated trajectory, colour-coded speed, and real-time force vectors, along with play/pause controls and a time slider for scrubbing through the motion
